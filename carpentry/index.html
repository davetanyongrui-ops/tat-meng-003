Smart Kitchen Cabinets

Kitchen Systems That Pay for Themselves in Convenience

A well-designed kitchen saves you minutes every day. Over a year, those minutes accumulate into hours of frictionless living. Our pull-out systems, corner carousel units, and concealed compartments transform wasted dead space into functional storage.

Concealed pantry units with adjustable shelving and integrated power tracks
Corner carousel mechanisms that rotate heavy loads smoothly
Soft-close hydraulic damping on every drawer and door hinge
Full-height unit configurations maximizing vertical storage for 3-room to 5-room layouts
Integrated appliance housings with ventilation clearance built in

Kitchen Material Finishes

Laminate

High-pressure laminate surfaces in 200+ finishes from textured woodgrain to matte solid colors. Durable, scratch-resistant, easy to clean.

Most popular for HDB and EC kitchens

Acrylic Panel

High-gloss acrylic surfaces with seamless edge-band profiles. Mirror-like finish that amplifies light in compact kitchens. Available in pure white, pearl, and metallic tones.

Premium option for modern aesthetics

Melamine Board

Engineered wood surface with baked-on decorative pattern. Cost-effective solution with good UV resistance and consistent color across large panels.

Budget-conscious choice without compromise

Custom Wardrobes

Wardrobes Built for the Clothes You Actually Own

Most wardrobes on the market assume you dress like a stranger. We start every project by understanding your wardrobe: how many long garments, how many accessories, whether you prefer drawn or hung storage. The result fits your life exactly.

Walk-in closet systems with back-to-wall configurations
Sliding door mechanisms with 4-track soft-close glide systems
Pull-out accessory trays: tie racks, watch drawers, belt rails, ring trays
Concealed hamper bins and ironing board housings in cabinet frames
Integrated internal lighting triggered by door motion sensors

Wardrobe Configurations

Hinged Door

Traditional swing-door configuration. Full access to interior on opening. Ideal for bedrooms with adequate clearance space.

Sliding Door

Space-saving glide mechanism. Two or four panel configurations. Mirror finish options available for compact rooms.

Open Wardrobe

Frame-based open system with hanging rails and shelving brackets. Industrial aesthetic without enclosure limitations.

Pivot Wardrobe

Rotating corner unit configuration. Four-faced access from a central spine. Space-efficient for awkward room corners.

Wall Panel Systems

Structural millwork panels installed over existing walls create architectural depth. Woodgrain veneers, lacquered surfaces, and fabric-wrapped acoustic panels turn flat walls into layered design statements.

Wood veneer panels: natural timber grain laid over engineered substrates
Lacquered gloss panels: high-gloss polyurethane finish in any RAL color
Fabric-wrapped acoustic panels: sound-dampening felt surfaces for media walls
Fluted millwork panels: vertical groove profiles creating rhythm and depth

Interior Doors & Millwork

Flush Panel Doors

Flat-surface interior doors with concealed hinges and push-to-open mechanisms. Clean lines without visible hardware.

French Groove Doors

Vertical groove profiles on door faces creating traditional paneling effect at engineered cost. Available in woodgrain or lacquer finishes.

Bi-Fold Partition Doors

Full-height folding partitions separating study areas from living spaces while maintaining visual continuity and natural light flow.
