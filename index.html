Corner Carousel Systems

Soft-Close Drawer Networks

LED Cornice Lighting

Where Light Becomes Architecture

Lighting shouldn't just illuminate a room — it should transform it. Our concealed LED cornices eliminate harsh overhead glare and create ambient layers that make spaces feel larger, warmer, and more intentional.

Perimeter Wash Lighting

Full-room ambient glow along the ceiling perimeter that doubles height perception and eliminates shadow corners.

Recessed Profile Channels

CNC-extruded aluminum channels with frosted diffusers create seamless linear light lines in walls and ceilings.

Smart Color Temperature

Tunable lighting systems shift from 2700K warmth to 4000K coolness, adapting to time of day throughout the house.

Architectural Accent Lines

Thin LED strips embedded into shelving niches, display areas, and architectural millwork for gallery-level drama.

Wet Works Support

Flooring, Plumbing, and Structural Prep for Seamless Integration

Great furniture needs sound prep work beneath it. We provide wet works coordination to ensure that what we build sits on level, waterproofed surfaces with proper access points for plumbing and electrical rough-in.

Subfloor Preparation

Leveling screed, moisture barrier installation, underlayment prep ensuring your carpentry rests on a perfectly flat surface.

Plumbing Rough-In Coordination

We work alongside plumbers to ensure kitchen cabinets and bathroom vanities align with pipe locations without modification on-site.

Tile and Flooring Transition Planning

Precise height calculations between tilework, hardwood, and carpet transitions for crisp sightlines under our built-ins.
