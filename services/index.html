Smart Storage Solutions

Hidden Compartments and Organizers That Redesign Your Lifestyle

Storage is not just about capacity. It is about the friction of finding items, the mental load of organizing, and the space lost to awkward gaps between furniture and walls. Our smart storage solutions operate invisibly until you need them, then deliver everything at arm's reach.

Concealed Appliance Cupboards

Full-height units that hide rice cookers, toasters, and kettles behind seamless panel fronts. Interior power tracks keep appliances charged without visible cables.

Blind Corner Optimization

Magic corner carousel units with tiered wire baskets that rotate 180 degrees, converting dead corner space into full-access shelving for pantry items.

Toe-Kick Drawers

Invisible storage hidden beneath your kitchen counter. Perfect stashing of baking trays, placemats, and flat-pack boxes that have no other good home.

Staircase Integration Storage

Under-stair cabinet systems in landed properties with pull-out drawer arrays maximizing every inch of traditionally wasted vertical space.

LED Cornice Lighting

Architectural Lighting That Transforms Room Character

Concealed LED cornices create the illusion of floating ceilings, wash walls with color, and eliminate the harsh shadows that standard downlights cast across your furniture. Our installations work across HDB soffit lines, condo drop-down beams, and landed property ceiling heights.

Perimeter Wash

LED strips installed along the junction of walls and ceiling create a continuous glow that visually raises ceiling height by up to 30 centimeters.

Recessed Groove Profile

CNC-extruded aluminum channels with prismatic diffusers creating razor-sharp linear light lines that cut through darkness like a blade.

Tunable White

Smart CCT controls shifting lighting temperature from warm 2700K in evenings to cool 4000K in mornings. App-controlled or automated via sensor scheduling.

Architectural Accent

LED channels embedded inside shelving niches, display areas, and millwork profiles delivering gallery-level accent illumination on your collections.

Extruded Aluminum Profiles

CNC-machined channel bodies with frosted polycarbonate diffusers. Available in straight runs up to 3 meters with corner radii for interior angles.

Finishes: Brushed aluminum, matte black, gold anodized

LED Module Configurations

SMD 2835 and 5054 LED modules offering 160-220 lumens per watt. Dimmable via DALI, DMX, or proprietary smart home protocols.

Color temperatures: 2700K, 3000K, 3500K, 4000K available

Wet Works Support

The Foundation Behind Every Successful Renovation

The quality of our carpentry is only as good as the surfaces it sits on. We coordinate floor leveling, waterproofing verification, and plumbing rough-in to ensure that what we build rests on properly prepared substrates with all utility points exposed before furniture installation.

Wet Works Coordination Scope

Subfloor Leveling

Self-leveling screed application ensuring substrates are within 2mm over 2m tolerance. Critical for kitchen and wardrobe installations requiring flush cabinet bottoms.

Waterproofing

Bathroom and wet area membrane application with 24-hour flood testing before any carpentry can be commissioned in adjacent spaces.

Plumbing Rough-In Access

Strategic access panel positioning behind cabinetry ensuring plumbers can service all connections without dismantling furniture later.
