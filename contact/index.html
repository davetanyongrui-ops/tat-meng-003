Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a kitchen cabinet installation cost?

Our kitchen packages start from $6,500 for basic HDB galley kitchens and scale up based on cabinet lineal meters, material choice (laminate / acrylic / melamine), hardware specifications, and complexity of storage integrations. We provide itemized quotes within our consultation so there are never any cost surprises.

What is the typical lead time from consultation to installation?

Standard projects take between 4-8 weeks depending on scope. A compact kitchen in an HDB BTO can be completed in approximately 4 weeks from proposal sign-off, while full-home packages for landed properties typically require 6-10 weeks including wet works coordination.

Do you offer after-installation service or warranty?

Yes. Every installation carries a minimum 12-month warranty on joinery integrity and hardware operation. Extended warranty packages up to 5 years are available at the time of project sign-off. We also provide one free adjustment visit at the 6-month mark to address any natural settling.

Can you work within an existing renovation timeline?

Absolutely. Our fabrication process is fully coordinated with your main contractor's schedule (for HDB contractors, property developers' packages, or independent renovation teams). We typically fit our workshop production around the wet works completion milestone to begin installation on site.

Is site preparation included in your quote?

Our quotes cover carpentry installation specifically. We can coordinate wet works -- floor leveling, waterproofing verification, plumbing rough-in access panel integration -- as a supplementary scope item or work alongside your existing renovation contractor's prep work with advance site inspection.
